About This Project

In 1980, Ray Bradbury spoke to a group of students at Sherwood Oaks Experimental College on a panel about screenwriting. His advice to them was to read one short story, one poem, and one essay every night for 1,000 nights.

This website was built by front-and-back-end duo Devon Lukasiewicz and Joe Mandozzi.

All works are sourced from Project Gutenberg, Standard Ebooks, and PoetryDB — sources that have already verified US public-domain status. If you would like to support this project, please consider donating to these organizations.
