About

Read one short story, one poem, and one essay every night for 1,000 nights. At the end of 1,000 nights you will be full of images, ideas, and feelings.

— Ray Bradbury

This site serves one carefully chosen public-domain short story, poem, and essay each day. Every work is real — nothing is AI-generated. The selection is deterministic: the same three works appear for every visitor on the same day.

All works are sourced from Project Gutenberg, Standard Ebooks, and PoetryDB — sources that have already verified US public-domain status.
